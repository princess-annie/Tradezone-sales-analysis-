## TradeZone Sales Performance & Strategic Growth Analysis

## Project Overview
This project delivers a data-driven diagnostic of TradeZone's sales operations between 2023 and 2024. 
Using PostgreSQL, the analysis investigates seller performance concentration, regional payment behaviour, and the relationship between product ratings
and revenue providing the Head of Growth and Head of Seller Operations with actionable recommendations to protect revenue and scale operations.

## Problem Statement
TradeZone was operating under a broad market growth strategy, but the data told a different story.
A small elite group of sellers was generating disproportionately more revenue than the rest of the seller base, creating an invisible concentration risk. At the same time, regional payment preferences were limiting digital transaction growth in the north, and mid-rated products were outperforming high-rated ones, a signal that quality perception and pricing were misaligned.

The core question: Where is revenue actually coming from, and what is at risk if nothing changes?

## Key Questions used to get this insights
1. Top 5 states by number of new customer sign-ups in 2024.
2. Top 10 products by total revenue in 2024.
3. Top 20 sellers with fastest average fuillment.
4. Quarterly revenue across 2023 and 2024.
5. Segment customers based on their total spend in 2024 based on three groups
   - High Spenders
   - Medium Spenders
   - Low Spenders
6. Payment Method Preferences by State
7. Review Ratings and Sales Performance
8. Top Seller Bonus Qualification
